JAKARTA

Jakarta is the first place where we first met in person. It's a short stay but one of the memorable one. Jakarta is a busy place since it's the capital of Indonesia and the most busy and developed City.

We have explored the city a little bit. Our taxi experience is not that good since the driver was trying to make us pay more since he knows we are not locals. I think this is the problem with most of the developed cities, when it comes to tourists, taxi drivers tend to take advantage of the tourists, which is so sad.
